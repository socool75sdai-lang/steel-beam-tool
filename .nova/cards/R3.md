## Audit finding
The six `<ReferenceLine>` components were already rendered unconditionally — guarded only by the
early `if (!results || !diagrams) return` at the top of the component, with no pass/fail wrapper.
The HANDOVER's example code referenced fields that do not exist on the real type
(`results.momentPass`, `results.deflGQPass`); copying it verbatim would break the build. The real
type uses `results.passes.*`, used only for stroke colour — not presence.

The genuine reason lines could be invisible is axis auto-scaling: a capacity line far outside the
data range gets clipped. Fixed by adding `ifOverflow="extendDomain"` to all six capacity lines so
they always remain on-axis. Stroke colour still reflects pass/fail; presence does not.
